Vegetarian Tacos

4.3 stars

Ingredients

Amount Food Item
1 cup Green beans
3 ears Fresh corn
2 cups Tomotatillo salsa
8 Corn torillas
0.5 cups Cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Blanch the green beans
  2. Grill the green beans and the ears of corn
  3. Chop the green beans
  4. Slice the corn off the cob
  5. Grill the torillas
  6. Assemble the tacos with veggies, salsa, and cheese
